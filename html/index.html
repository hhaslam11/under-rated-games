Grand Theft Auto, Battlefield, Call of Duty... They're all good games and all; however, some games just deserve a little bit more love...

Here's a list of games that just aren't as popular & well known as they should be.

Shelter

Shelter description placeholder

Graphics

Shelter has a very unique hand-drawn-like art style to it.

Gameplay

Mostly interesting and interactive gameplay, with an even variety of puzzles, hiding from enemies, and trying to find food for your children. Sometimes gameplay can be a bit repetative or frustating, though.

Story

Shelter has a emotional linear story with an open world feel, different life-threatening events could happen, and depending on how well you play the game, your cubs will either live or die.

Shelter 2

Shelter 2 description placeholder

Graphics

Shelter 2 Graphics are just like Shelter 1; Unique, yet beautiful

Gameplay

Shelter 2 focuses on an open-world gameplay, the goal is to keep your kittens alive in a fairly large world full of different threats/rewards.

Story

Shelter 2 took away most of the story aspect that Shelter 1 had, to make a more open-world game. There's very little story, other than short cutscenes at the beggining and end of the game.

Paws

Paws description placeholder

Graphics

Once again, Paws doesnt disapoint with their one-of-a-kind art style, just like the previous two games.

Gameplay

Full of puzzles to solve and threats to avoid, Paws will keep you entertained throughout the entire (short) game.

Story

Paws goes back to Shelter 1's style with a linear story. It has an interesting, emotional, and suspensful story.

Firewatch

Firewatch description placeholder

Graphics

Defintely one of the most gorgoues, atmospheric games on this list. Firewatch is worth playing just for the graphics themself.

Gameplay

Often boring gameplay, which mostly consists of walking back and forth from point-a to point-b. The game is open world, but theres not too much to explore. Same old trees and mountains throughtout the entire map.

Story

Firewatch has a very suspensful and exciting story, with great character development. However, the ending of the game was quite dissapointing and uneventful.

Life is Strange

Life is Strange description placeholder

Graphics

Decent cartoon-like graphics, but nothing too special

Gameplay

Gameplay is interesting enough to not be considered boring, but sometimes the puzzles can be stretched out too long. Plenty of stuff to explore throughout the map of Arcadia Bay.

Story

I can not stress how amazing the story in this game is. Starts out with peaceful, typical highschool drama, but gets really dark really fast. A ton of surprising twists in the story.

The Walking Dead Game by Telltale

The Walking Dead Game description placeholder

Graphics

Comic like graphics, but could be better

Gameplay

Often boring, long, and frusterating- but can also be fun and exciting at times.

Story

Another suspenful, exciting, and emotional story. You never know what'll happen next; who'll die next; etc.

Prison Architect

Prison Architect description placeholder

Graphics

OK graphics, very simple, and sometimes look awkward/strange

Gameplay

A ton of events/situations to keep you entertained for ten's of hours. Building prisons is fun, and keeping your prisoners in control is even more fun.

Story

Has a pretty short campaign mode, but it's extremely boring and not even worth playing through. Stick to building your own prisons. Can't really expect much of a story from a Sims-like sandbox game, though.

A Story About My Uncle

A Story About My Uncle description placeholder

Graphics

Average graphics for an indie game. Nothing special to see here.

Gameplay

Entertaining platformer, fun puzzles, and grapling hook! Can't go wrong with a grapling hook. Has time trails too, for after you finished the pretty short campaign.

Story

Fairly good, well told story. Nothing that'll leave you thinking about it though.
